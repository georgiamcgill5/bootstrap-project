Home

The Animal Protection Society is a non-profit enterprise that aims to rescue homesless and injured strays off of the streets.
We rehabilitate them, reintroduce them to other dogs and humans, and rehome them to loving homes once they have reached their full health.

Adopt

Adopting a dog today can mean happiness for you, your family and your new dog. By adopting through APS, you are not only saving one dogs life, but also contibuting to the saving of many other dogs out there that are yet to be rescued.

Donate

We are a non-profit business, which means all funds from dog adoptions and all donations go towards new equipment, dog food and toys, and petrol for transport to rescue stray dogs. Your donation could help save a dogs life.
