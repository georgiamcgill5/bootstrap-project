Adopt a Dog

It is our mission to provide you with a perfectly healthy and social dog, so that they won't ever have to spend another night on the streets. All dogs will be registered, microchipped, desexed, vaccinated and flea and worm treated as part of the adoption process.
All proceeds go towards new equipment, dog food and toys, and petrol for transport to rescue other abandoned and stray dogs.
All customers wishing to adopt must undergo a compulsary background check, agree to our terms and conditions and fill out some documentation before you can make your payment and take your dog home for the first time.

Olive

Olive is a German Shorthaired Pointer puppy. She is 18 weeks old. Olive has not been desexed, as she is too young. Olive is avaliable to be adopted but you will need to come back to us when the time comes for her to be desexed. We will do this for you, free of charge. Olive is an escape artist, so you will need to have a secure backyard. German Shorthaired Pointers generally have a lot of energy so you need to be able to spend a lot of time with her. As she is a puppy, she likes to chew things. She knows what she can and can't chew, but if she gets bored, she will chew anything - especially shoes! If Olive sounds like the puppy for you, please adopt her!
